The Burj Khalifa in Dubai, United Arab Emirates, is currently the tallest building in the world, standing at 828 meters (2,717 feet). It is a testament to modern engineering and architectural innovation. Completed in 2010, the Burj Khalifa attracts millions of tourists who visit its observation decks for unparalleled views of Dubai's skyline.

For similar architectural achievements, see the Eiffel Tower and the Empire State Building.
