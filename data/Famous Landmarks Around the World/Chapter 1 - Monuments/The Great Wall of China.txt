The Great Wall of China stretches over 13,000 miles and is one of the most iconic historical landmarks in the world. Originally built to protect China from invasions, the wall consists of walls, watchtowers, and trenches. It showcases incredible ancient Chinese engineering and is a UNESCO World Heritage site.

The Great Wall is located near Beijing and is one of the most popular tourist destinations in China.
