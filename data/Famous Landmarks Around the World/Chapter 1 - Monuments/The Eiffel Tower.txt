The Eiffel Tower, located in Paris, France, is one of the most famous landmarks in the world. It was constructed in 1889 for the World's Fair and stands at 324 meters tall. The tower is a symbol of France and one of the most visited paid monuments in the world. Visitors can take an elevator ride to the top for stunning views of Paris, including landmarks like the Notre-Dame Cathedral and the Louvre Museum.
